Pakistan flood map project

Data visualization of the devastating floods that impacted Pakistan in 2022

Article

Description

Part of my job at Tenko as a data and modeling engineer was to develop a tool to automatically download satellite data and calculate flood maps from this raw satellite data. The tool was able to download data and generate aggregated flood maps for a country such as Pakistan over a period of one year in a matter of minutes. This map was produced using python, geemap, and Rasterio.

In the article you can see the important difference between the floods in 2021 and 2022.

The code source is property of Tenko.
